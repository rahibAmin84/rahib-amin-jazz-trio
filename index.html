About Us

Rahib Amin

Rahib is a dynamic jazz guitarist shaped by Hindustani music, Arabic melodies, avant-garde jazz, and American soul. From Saudi Arabia to the U.S., his sound blends diverse global influences.

Matt Hall

A double bassist with roots in Virginia and Istanbul, Matt is a seasoned performer and collaborator with jazz legends. A strong melodic force in the trio.

Instagram · Webpage

Karl Tietze

A graduate of Loyola New Orleans, Karl brings over 15 years of groove, soul, and precision. From New Orleans to global stages, his drumming anchors the trio’s sound.
