## Future photo wishlist

When convenient, add:

1. One warm professional portrait — founder/operator feel, not stiff corporate.
2. One candid working photo — laptop, whiteboard, workshop, architecture diagram, or speaking moment.
3. One high-quality performance photo — guitar, expression, atmosphere.
4. One environmental image — York wall, jazz club, studio, Richmond, or a meaningful city texture.

## Rule of thumb

Use real, imperfect, emotionally credible photos over generic stock images. A personal brand site should feel like a person with a point of view, not a template.
