Music & Arts

Jazz guitar, improvisation, collaboration, and creative discipline.

My music work explores the relationship between structure and freedom — tradition and risk, listening and expression, precision and feeling.

Artist statement

Music as a living system.

Born in Saudi Arabia and shaped by a rich mix of musical traditions, my playing draws from Hindustani music, Arabic melodies, jazz, free improvisation, early American music, rock, soul, R&B, and bebop.

I approach music as a conversation: between musicians, audiences, traditions, places, and moments. Jazz has taught me how to listen deeply, respond honestly, and build structure without losing freedom.

That same discipline shapes how I think about teams, technology, and trust. The best systems leave room for judgment, timing, and human expression.
